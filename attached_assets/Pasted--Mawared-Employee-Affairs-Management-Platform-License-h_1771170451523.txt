**Simplifying Employee Management for Saudi Businesses**

Mawared is a comprehensive employee affairs management platform designed specifically for small to medium-sized businesses in Saudi Arabia. Built to comply with Saudi labor laws, Mawared streamlines attendance tracking, leave management, payroll processing, and employee requests through an intuitive mobile and web platform.

## ‚≠ê Key Features

### 1. GPS-Based Attendance Tracking
- **Geofencing Technology** - Employees can only clock in/out within designated work location radius (50-500m)
- **Single Device Enforcement** - Prevents time theft by restricting attendance to one device per employee
- **Real-Time Verification** - GPS coordinates and timestamps captured with each entry
- **Automatic Status Detection** - Flags late arrivals, early departures, and absences
- **Device Change Protection** - 14-day cooldown period with admin approval required

### 2. Comprehensive Leave Management
- **Saudi Labor Law Compliance** - Pre-configured leave types (Annual, Sick, Emergency, Hajj, Maternity, etc.)
- **Automatic Balance Tracking** - Real-time leave balance accrual and deduction
- **Multi-Level Approvals** - Configurable approval workflows per store
- **Leave Calendar** - Visual representation of team availability
- **Leave Forecasting** - Plan upcoming absences and team capacity

### 3. Employee Request System
- **Multiple Request Types** - Salary certificates, experience letters, housing allowances, bonuses, etc.
- **Intelligent Routing** - Requests automatically directed to appropriate approver (Store Owner, HR Team, or Auto-Approved)
- **Configurable Workflows** - Each store can customize approval sequences
- **Status Tracking** - Real-time updates on request progress
- **Document Generation** - Automated creation of official documents

### 4. Smart Payroll Processing
- **Attendance Integration** - Hours worked automatically calculated from attendance data
- **Deduction Management** - Late arrivals, early departures, and absences handled automatically
- **Leave Impact** - Unpaid leaves deducted, paid leaves processed correctly
- **Saudi Compliance** - GOSI, overtime, and end-of-service calculations
- **Payslip Generation** - Automated monthly payslips for all employees

### 5. Analytics & Reporting
- **Live Dashboards** - Real-time workforce metrics and KPIs
- **Attendance Reports** - Daily, weekly, monthly summaries with export to Excel/PDF
- **Leave Analytics** - Leave utilization, trends, and forecasting
- **Request Analytics** - Processing times, approval rates, bottlenecks
- **Custom Reports** - Build your own reports with flexible filters

### 6. Multi-Tenant Architecture
- **Store Isolation** - Complete data separation between different stores
- **Row-Level Security** - Database-level access control for maximum security
- **Scalable Design** - Supports unlimited stores and employees
- **VIP Accounts** - Custom features and limits for enterprise clients
